About the Game

KATIPUDROID is a 3D story-driven role-playing game that brings the heroism of the Katipunan to life. Step into the shoes of Macario Sakay, a barber, actor, and brave Katipunero who fights for freedom during the Spanish colonization of the Philippines.

Experience the struggles, sacrifices, and victories of Filipino revolutionaries through immersive missions, historical battles, and interactive storytelling. Explore 3D environments, fight Spanish forces, uncover secrets, and relive key moments that shaped the nation’s history.

Developed for Android, KATIPUDROID blends education and adventure, making Philippine history come alive through gameplay. Learn about the Katipunan, the rise of nationalism, and the fight for independence

KATIPUDROID is an action-packed 3D story-driven RPG that immerses players in the heroic journey of Macario Sakay, a brave Katipunero who fights for freedom during the Spanish colonization. The game unfolds across four thrilling chapters, each packed with three missions of intense combat, stealth challenges, and historical storytelling. From surviving oppression in Tondo to fighting alongside revolutionaries in the Battle of San Juan del Monte, players will relive the rise of the Katipunan and the struggle for independence.

  • Chapter 1
    Witness the cruelty of Spanish rule as Macario Sakay sees the suffering of his fellow Filipinos and takes his first stand against the Guardia Civil.
  • Chapter 2
    Macario officially joins the Katipunan, smuggles weapons, and faces betrayal as the revolution’s first sparks begin to ignite.
  • Chapter 3
    The Katipuneros tear their cedulas and rise against Spain. Macario joins the fight at the Battle of San Juan del Monte.
  • Chapter 4
    As division tears the Katipunan apart, Macario witnesses Bonifacio’s fall and fights his last battle for freedom at Biak-na-Bato.

Characters

Macario Sakay

Macario Sakay was a member of the Katipunan who upheld its ideals of Philippine independence even after Spain's defeat.

Andres Bonifacio

Andrés Bonifacio was the founder and leader of the Katipunan, a secret revolutionary society that fought for Philippine independence from Spanish colonial rule.

Tandang Sora

Tandang Sora, whose real name was Melchora Aquino, was known as the Mother of the Philippine Revolution for aiding members of the Katipunan. She provided food, shelter, and medical care to revolutionaries, earning deep respect despite being exiled by Spanish authorities for her support.

Emilio Aguinaldo

Emilio Aguinaldo was the first president of the Philippines and a key leader in the struggle for independence from both Spain and the United States.
